Prologue

Today is finally the time you have been waiting for, young wizard, your new master is waiting for you at the main district in the capital.

What was his name again? Please type in the name below.

But first, 

tasked you to acquire some reagents on your way to see him.

Now that you’re out of your house, you must choose where you will go.

You have bought the reagents needed and decide to spare some more time in another shop. There is no one at the counter.

You choose to browse the items in store and decide to look at one item in particular. The item is in fact a beautiful stone surrounded by a strange force that is compelling you to touch it.

You are now in a small patch of nature in the city. Gazing at the beautiful decor, you then confirm that you have every reagents 

wanted and asked for.

On your way through the tall grass you see from afar a gigantic six legged bear.

After taking some distance from the bear, you turn around to see if it is chasing you. However, you realize that the bear is even bigger than before, but strangely, at the same location.

Intrigued by this curious phenomenon, you decide to get a closer look to the unmoving bear. You approach cautiously and notice the closer you approach the six legged gigantic bear, the more it really looks like a little ball of fur.

Relieved by the discovery that the creature's large appearance was a magical defensive mechanism and was in fact closer to the size of a little chihuahua, you are surprise when it decide to charge you and grab your leg with its six clawed paws. It hurts you little more than paper cuts.

You decide against a violent solution.

While you struggle with the tiny six legged bear, you conjure one t-bone and throw it at the base of a tree near you. It took seconds for the furry ball to smell the tempting scent of raw meat and unleash its hold on you.

Going for the bait, you seize the opportunity to get away from it.

While the bear is still struggling to kill you and your leg, you quickly take off your magical hat and catch it with it.

Fortunately, the catch is successful. You decide to keep it as a pet.

Arriving near the main district, you hear a buzzing sound coming from the area ahead. You decide to find the source of the sound, leading you toward an area where, strangely, you are supposed to meet your master 

.
